Battle-At-Sea

A fun nostalgic game that pays homage to the classic BattleshipⓇ

I chose to create Battle-At-Sea because I love to see recreations and people’s personal take on classics. Therefore, I wanted to create my own version of Battleship.Playing different versions of Battleship online, I have yet to come across one that paid homage to retro video game styles. Therefore, my goals were not only to recreate game-play but to style it in a way that will give the players a sense of nostalgia. I wanted players to feel as if they were kids again at the arcade while playing my game - that was my number one priority.
Independent Project

Fetch!

A web app centered on finding and sharing Dog-Friendly locations around Chicago

I built this site because I love taking my dog with me wherever I go. I find it hard to sort through dog-friendly places at times. I also found it hard to find places that other pet parents consider dog-friendly. Therefore, I and my partner created an app that will allow users to share their favorite places to take their dog and only show dog-friendly establishments around Chicago.
